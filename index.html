Games rules

Game goal

Each player has a global and a current score. The goal is to be the first to have 100 global point

At each turn

  • The player has his current score reset. He can roll a dice as many times as he wants, the result will be added to his current score.

  • During his turn, he can "Hold", it means that the player will add his current score to his global score but his turn will end.

  • However if he get a 1, his current score goes back to 0 and his turn will end as well.
